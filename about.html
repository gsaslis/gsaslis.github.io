About me

I love the gift culture of Open Source. It is an aspect of society I value, that gives me hope for the future. So do people who volunteer. And people who are not afraid to laugh, who enjoy good food, get lost in music and value the person behind a good argument.


In terms of work:

After spending 10+ years working in telecoms in concurrency and distributed systems problems, I spent another ~8 at Red Hat, automating software releases and optimizing Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines. Nowadays, I am passionate about building a better home for the world's Open Source Software with Radicle.


I also love open space unconferences (so I help run SoCraTes Crete and JCrete.org) and meetups (I co-founded DevStaff and Heraklion Software Crafters and am nowadays co-organising Athens Software Crafters). There's a good chance you'll catch me at a SoCraTes conference around the world. ;)


Please ping me on one of the social channels at the top right of the page, if there's anything you want to discuss.
